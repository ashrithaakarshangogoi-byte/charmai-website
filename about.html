About Charm AI

Charm is an advanced voice-enabled AI assistant specifically designed for aviation environments, with a focus on supporting pilots during critical flight operations. Developed by Team Aviators, Charm utilizes cutting-edge artificial intelligence models through the OpenRouter API, integrating capabilities like natural language understanding, real-time audio interaction, and aviation-specific decision support.

The assistant is built using a combination of modern web technologies including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with back-end integrations powered by Python and RESTful API calls. Charm supports both aviation-specific queries (like controlling or troubleshooting the F-117 Nighthawk) and open-domain interactions, making it versatile and intelligent.

Designed with a futuristic UI, Charm not only provides fast and reliable voice responses but also features an immersive user experience with animated visuals, video backgrounds, and toggleable aviation modes. Charm is optimized for hands-free usage, making it a vital co-pilot in digital form, ideal for single-device cockpits.

About the F-117 Nighthawk

The F-117 Nighthawk is a stealth aircraft known for its unique, sharp-angled design that helps it avoid radar detection. It was built by Lockheed for the U.S. Air Force and became the world’s first operational aircraft made specifically for stealth missions.

Although it doesn't look like a traditional fighter jet, the F-117 was designed to fly deep into enemy territory at night, strike with precision, and return without being detected. It was mainly used in secret missions and became famous for its role in real combat situations like Operation Desert Storm.

With its futuristic look and radar-evading features, the F-117 is considered one of the most iconic aircraft ever built. Its design has inspired many aviation and defense technologies around the world.
